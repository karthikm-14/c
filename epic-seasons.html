League Points System

Every sale and achievement contributes to your League Points, which are earned automatically for each sale and performance metric you achieve.

D2D Points Breakdown

  • Fiber Orders: 1 point per order
  • Non-Fiber Attachments Sold: 1 point per item
  • Holiday Acceleration: 3 points per order
  • Recruits Hired: 1 point per recruit's first 10 orders

MDU Points Breakdown

  • 1GB+ Fiber Unit Orders: 3 points per order
  • < 1GB Fiber Unit (incl copper) Orders: 1 point per order
  • Non-Fiber Attachments Sold: 2 points per attachment

BOOSTERS vs. League Points Clarification

01
League Points

Intangible points used to qualify for rewards and tiers.

02
Boosters

Tangible rewards (cash, swag, trips) for winning competitions.
